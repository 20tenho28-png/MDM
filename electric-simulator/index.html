⚡ Welcome to the Electric Simulator

Build DC circuits with EU (IEC 60617) schematic symbols and watch them work in real time. Made for technicians and new hires — nothing here can break.

Add a partDrag it from the left panel onto the grid, or pick it and draw between two grid points. Wire it upPick Wire (key 2) and drag between terminals. Current flows as soon as the loop is closed. OperateClick a switch to open or close it. Lamps light up according to real power. EditClick a part to change its value (EU presets included), rotate R, or remove Del. Move aroundDrag empty grid to pan · mouse wheel to zoom · 0 fits the circuit. UndoCtrl+Z undo · Ctrl+Y redo. Experiment freely. Read valuesHover any part for volts, amps and watts. A red flash + warning means short circuit.
