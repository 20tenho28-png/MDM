⚡ Welcome!

This is a safe playground for building electric circuits. Nothing can break, nothing can hurt you — try everything.

The one ruleElectricity only flows in a complete loop: from the battery's +, through your parts, back to −. BuildDrag parts from the left panel onto the grid. Connect them by dragging wires from their end points — wires bend around corners by themselves. Try itClick the switch in the example circuit. The lamp lights, and the little orange dots show the electricity flowing. ColoursUse real EU wire colours: brown from +, blue back to −, green/yellow for earth. Look insidePoint at any part to see its numbers. Click a part to change it. The Voltage colours button paints wires red (near +) to blue (near −). No fearCtrl+Z undoes anything. If something glows red: that's a short circuit — the current has found a path with nothing to slow it down.
